Description

This pasta alla vodka is an easy recipe to throw together for a quick dinner any night of the week â€” the sauce c an be made while you cook the penne pasta. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese to serve. Delicious!

Ingredients :

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic,minced
  • 1(28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh Basil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup vodka
  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 1 pint heavy cream

Steps :

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic; cook and stir until tender, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in tomatoes, breaking up a bit with a fork. Stir in basil, salt, and pepper; simmer for 15 minutes. Stir in vodka and cook 15 minutes more.
  2. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain
  3. stir cream into sauce and cook 10 minutes more.Toss with hot pasta
